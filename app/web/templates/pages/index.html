Reviving Ancient Wisdom for Modern Minds

Inspired by the legacy of King Ashoka and the esteemed Nalanda University, the Ashoka Buddhist Foundation, founded by Bhikkhu Shripad, aims to embody their ideals. We are committed to reviving and preserving ancient Buddhist culture, emphasizing the values of compassion, wisdom, and harmony for individuals and society as a whole.

Our Core Pillars

Education & Research

Promoting in-depth study and understanding of Buddhist philosophy, history, and practices through courses, workshops, and scholarly exchange.

Meditation & Mindfulness

Offering guidance and retreats in various meditation techniques to cultivate inner peace, clarity, and emotional well-being.

Community & Service

Fostering a supportive Sangha and encouraging compassionate action and service for the betterment of all beings.

Embark on Your Journey

Whether you are new to Buddhist teachings or a seasoned practitioner, the Ashoka Buddhist Foundation offers resources and a community to support your path.
